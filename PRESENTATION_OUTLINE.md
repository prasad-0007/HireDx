# 📊 HireDx Presentation Deck Outline

Use this outline to build your 5-10 slide PPT or PDF deck. Each slide is designed for maximum impact and clear storytelling.

---

### Slide 1: Title & Vision
- **Header**: HireDx
- **Sub-header**: The AI "Autopsy" for Your Job Interviews.
- **Visual**: Premium Logo (centered).
- **Key Message**: Transforming rejection from a mystery into a roadmap for growth.

### Slide 2: The Problem: The "Feedback Vacuum"
- **Bullet Points**:
    - Candidates receive generic "We've decided to move forward with others" emails.
    - Zero visibility into *why* they failed.
    - Silent rejection signals (pacing, filler words, lack of depth) are hard to self-diagnose.
- **Pain Point**: "You can't fix what you can't see."

### Slide 3: The Solution: Multimodal Diagnosis
- **Header**: Meet HireDx.
- **Content**: A platform that treats every interview like a data-rich session.
- **Key Capabilities**: 
    - Behavioral pattern detection.
    - Speech structural analysis.
    - Real-time confidence tracking.
- **Result**: Instant, brutal, and actionable feedback.

### Slide 4: Unique Feature — The Rejection Heatmap
- **Visual**: Screenshot of the "Executive Overview" timeline.
- **Value**: A chronological log of exactly when interest was lost. 
- **Details**: Red flags for filler word spikes, long pauses, and evasive answers.

### Slide 5: Unique Feature — Dynamic Action Roadmap
- **Visual**: Screenshot of the "Action Roadmap" selector.
- **Innovation**: No static advice. 
- **Content**: Choose from **Intensive**, **Balanced**, or **Polish** tracks. 
- **Killer Feature**: **Custom 1-90 Day Sprints** that generate unique weekly tasks automatically.

### Slide 6: Unique Feature — Targeted Practice Bank
- **Header**: Closing the Loop.
- **Visual**: Screenshot of the "Practice Bank" cards.
- **Mechanism**: AI generates 10 unique drill questions based *only* on your specific weaknesses from the previous session.

### Slide 7: Technical Architecture
- **Tech Stack Icons**: Next.js, Supabase, Google Gemini 2.5.
- **Process Flow**:
    1. User uploads media.
    2. Gemini Multimodal Analysis (Native audio/video processing).
    3. Failure JSON persisted to Supabase.
    4. React dashboard renders interactive insights.

### Slide 8: Privacy First: Zero-Retention
- **Header**: Your Data is Your Own.
- **Commitment**: Audio/Video files are processed in real-time and deleted immediately.
- **Architecture**: We store only the analysis (the "What"), never the recording (the "Who").

    - **Live Safety Net**: A real-time overlay during mock interviews and real calls.

    - **Executive Coaching**: Comparison against industry benchmarks (FAANG vs. Startups).
